Rules of the Game Updated

Join / Create
Join a league or start one with friends. Picks lock at episode start each week.
How You Win
Be the last Active player. If multiple survive to the end, highest total score wins.
Weekly Picks
Choose 3: Safe, Voted Out, and Immunity. Optional: risk points & parlay.
Making Picks
  1. Safe Pick: Any contestant you believe will not be eliminated this week. You cannot reuse the same contestant as safe in multiple weeks.
  2. Voted Out Pick: The contestant you think will be eliminated this week.
  3. Immunity Pick: The contestant (or tribe name pre-merge) you think wins immunity this week.
  4. Week 1 Safeguard: Nobody can be exiled in Week 1, even if their Safe Pick goes home. You still score points normally.
  5. Immunity Idols: Correctly predicting Voted Out earns you an Immunity Idol. Using an idol protects you from exile if your Safe Pick is eliminated (you still make other picks and earn points). If you miss a week entirely, one idol will auto-burn to protect you.
Status Flow
Active
Everyone starts here. If your Safe Pick is eliminated in Week 2+ (and you didn't use an idol), you are exiled.
Exiled
You can still play and earn points normally. Warning: If your Safe Pick is eliminated again while exiled (and you don't use an idol), you are permanently eliminated. You may return from exile at any time for 5 points (button on league page).
Eliminated
Permanent. Occurs when your Safe Pick fails twice (once to get exiled, once while exiled). Your past points remain on the leaderboard; no further picks allowed.
Scoring & Risk
Base Points (per week)
  • Safe Pick correct: +1
  • Safe Pick incorrect: 0 (or exiled/eliminated if no idol)
  • Voted Out correct: +3 + earn an Idol
  • Voted Out incorrect: 0
  • Immunity correct: +2
  • Immunity incorrect: 0

  • Weekly cap: Risk up to 3 points per week across both wagers.

Rules of the Game

Welcome to Survivor survivor pool. Here are the rules:

  1. Join a league or create your own.
  2. The winner is the person who lasts longest in the game, points are a tiebreker.
  3. Each week, you choose 3 contestants on the show.
  4. Each week, you can earn up to 3 points. Each correct guess earns you 1 point.
  5. Select one contestant as your "Safe Pick". This is any contestant currently on the show you think will not be eliminated.
  6. Select the contestant who you think will be voted out on the show that week.
  7. Select an Immunity Challenge winner.
  8. If your "Safe Pick" is eliminated, you are eliminated from the game.
  9. If you are elminated, it is possible to return once, by correctly choosing the Immunity challenge winner 5 times.
  10. If you are eliminated, you are allowed to keep playing to earn points, but you will lose 1 point on incorrect safe picks.
  11. If you correctly guess the contestant who was eliminated on the show, you earn an immunity idol.
  12. Use immunity idols to protect yourself from elimination.
  13. Picks lock when the weekly episode starts.
  14. Track your scores on the leaderboard!

For detailed rules, refer to the league-specific guidelines or contact your league admin.
